Privacy Policy

Introduction

This Privacy Policy governs the manner in which PDF Scanner Pro collects, uses, maintains, and discloses information collected from users (each, a "User") of the PDF Scanner Pro application.

Permissions

PDF Scanner Pro requires the following permissions:

  • Camera: This permission is required to capture images and scan documents using the device camera.
  • Storage: This permission is necessary to save the scanned images to the device's storage.

No other permissions are requested, and PDF Scanner Pro does not access or collect any personal information from users.

Data Usage

The images scanned using PDF Scanner Pro are stored locally on the user's device. The app does not transmit or store any scanned images externally.

Changes to this Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Users will be notified of any changes through app notifications or on our website.
